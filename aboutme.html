About Me

Welcome! My name is Megan Thomas. I am a software engineer with professional experience in cloud computing, web applications development, computer architecture, and automation. Explore and get to know me outside of my professional work.

I love plants. No matter what room you enter in my home, there are always a few house plants there to greet you, if that was not obvious already. Plant care is a special skill - many people claim to not have a green thumb. I will admit it takes some patience, willingness to learn, critical analysis, and thorough planning for your plants to thrive. However, it is not as difficult as many cares to believe.

I have the same mindset when it comes to architecting computer systems and developing software. The more time you spend growing your systems, nurturing your skills and planning thoroughly the more your systems will thrive. And in turn, your admiration for technology may just bloom as well. (Sorry for so many plant double entendres - I couldn't help myself.)

Most recently, I put that mindset to work as an Enterprise Tools and Automation Engineer for 3M Technology Transformation Services. My main responsibilities included designing, developing, and deploying a variety of enterprise-scale applications and automation on-premise and in the AWS Cloud. The languages I primarily used were PowerShell, SQL, VB.Net, and Python but as with many IT positions we were expected to teach ourselves new languages and services on a whim. The most valuable thing I gained from this position is learning how to learn in a fast-paced environment.
